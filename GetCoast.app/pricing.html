Have a question? We might have answered it below

  • Is the Free Plan free forever?

    Yep, that’s right! Our Free Plan let’s you identify influencers and streamline your influencer marketing campaigns. It’s like a free trial, but it doesn’t end!

  • Do I need to enter my credit card details to sign up?

    Nope! When you sign up, you’ll automatically be placed on our Free Plan and you can upgrade your account at any time.

  • What are my payment options?

    We currently accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover and Diners Club payments from customers worldwide. We currently do not support invoicing terms for subscriptions.

  • Will I be locked into a long-term contract?

    No! Coast is a month-to-month subscription, so you can downgrade (and then upgrade again) at any time.

  • If I need to, how do I downgrade my subscription?

    You can downgrade to our Basic Plan or Free Plan at any time. Here are the steps to downgrade your subscription. Here are the steps to downgrade your subscription. If you downgrade your subscription during an existing billing period, you will remain on your current plan until the end of the billing period. At the start of the new billing period you will be downgraded as per your request. We don’t prorate for partial months.

  • Does my subscription include payments to influencers for involvement in my campaigns?

    No. Your Coast subscription gives you assess to the Coast platform to identify influencers and run your campaign. Influencer payments are an additional cost.

  • Do I have to pay influencers when they participate in my campaign?

    Yes. Coast is for paid campaigns only, so you need to have budget to pay influencers when they participate in your campaign. You cannot run a contra campaign or exchange a product for a post on Coast.

  • Do you offer discounted pricing for start ups or not-for-profits?

    We’re happy you asked. We do offer a discount for start-ups and not-for-profits - please email hello@Coast.com for more information.

  • Have a question you don’t see here?

    Our support team is available 8.30am to 5.30pm AEST. We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours on business days. You can also visit our help center.
